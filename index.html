Our Mission

Preserving & Sharing Sikh Heritage

Vidhia is a free, open-access digital library dedicated to sharing resources about Sikhi with anyone seeking to learn, study, or deepen their understanding of this vibrant tradition.

We host over 526 PDF texts, scholarly works, and historical documents spanning Gurbani, theology, philosophy, history, and biography — all freely accessible without registration.
